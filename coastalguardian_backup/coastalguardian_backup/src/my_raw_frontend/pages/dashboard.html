About Coastal Guardian

Discover our mission, meet our team, and get answers to common questions about our Web3 marine conservation platform.

Our Mission

Coastal Guardian is revolutionizing marine conservation through blockchain technology and community engagement. We believe that every conservation action, no matter how small, contributes to the health of our oceans.

Our platform uses soulbound tokens to create permanent, verifiable records of conservation efforts, ensuring transparency and accountability while rewarding those who dedicate their time to protecting marine ecosystems.

By combining cutting-edge Web3 technology with proven conservation science, we're building a global community of ocean guardians committed to preserving marine biodiversity for future generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are soulbound tokens? +
Soulbound tokens (SBTs) are non-transferable digital certificates that permanently record your conservation achievements. Unlike regular NFTs, they cannot be sold or transferred, making them perfect for representing personal accomplishments and contributions.
How do I earn CGT tokens? +
You earn Coastal Guardian Tokens (CGT) by participating in verified conservation activities such as mangrove planting, beach cleanups, water quality monitoring, and educational outreach. Each action is verified and recorded on the blockchain.
Is the platform free to use? +
Yes! Coastal Guardian is completely free to use. We believe conservation should be accessible to everyone. You only need a Web3 wallet to connect and start earning tokens for your conservation efforts.
How are conservation actions verified? +
We use a combination of GPS tracking, photo verification, community validation, and partnerships with local conservation organizations to ensure all reported actions are legitimate and impactful.
Can I participate without crypto knowledge? +
Our platform is designed to be user-friendly for everyone. We provide step-by-step guides for setting up a wallet and participating in conservation activities, regardless of your Web3 experience.
What impact does my participation have? +
Every action contributes to real conservation outcomes. Your participation helps restore marine habitats, protect endangered species, improve water quality, and build resilient coastal communities while creating a permanent record of positive environmental impact.
